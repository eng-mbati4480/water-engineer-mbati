## Setup Instructions

### 1. **Add Your Photo**
Replace `ENG.MBATI.PNG` with your profile photo:
- Recommended size: 400x400 pixels (square aspect ratio)
- Format: PNG, JPG, or JPEG
- Will be displayed as circular image in hero section

### 2. **Add Background Video**
Replace `hero1.mp4` with your water engineering video:
- **Duration**: 10-30 seconds (loops automatically)
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 or higher
- **Format**: MP4 (primary) + WebM (optional)
- **File Size**: Keep under 10MB
- **Content Suggestions**:
  - Water flow and streams
  - Irrigation system in action
  - Engineering work or construction
  - Urban/agricultural water projects
  - Solar water pumps in operation

**Free Video Sources:**
- Pexels.com
- Pixabay.com
- Unsplash.com
- YouTube (water engineering documentaries)

### 3. **Customize Gallery Items**
Edit `gallery.html` to add your projects:
- Update image file names
- Add project titles and descriptions
- Include location, year, and impact metrics
- Replace video with your own project footage

### 4. **Update Contact Information**
In `index.html`, update:
- Email address
- Phone number
- Physical location
- WhatsApp number (for chat button)

### 5. **Open the Website**
- Double-click `index.html` to open in browser
- Or use live server for development
